One Print One Tree Program (OPOT)

Reduce.Reuse.Grow (RRG) has partnered with Canva to plant a tree for every print made on their platform. This initiative, known as One Print, One Tree (OPOT), aims to restore degraded landscapes and sequester carbon through reforestation projects throughout Asia, Africa and South America.
