Stakeholder Rights, Interests, and Relevance to the Project
Friends of the Usambara They will run on-the-ground operations, handle the FPIC process with local stakeholders, work in nursery, planting, and monitoring. They will manage hiring of field employees and receive the bulk of profits from carbon credit sales.
Tanzania Forest Services Agency
(Primarily: Baga FR, Bombo Makole FR, Chambogo FR, Magamba FR, Mkussu FR, Mtumbi FR, Ndelemai FR) 		Identified through RRG’s on-the-ground partners (FOU). They own the forest reserve land for project activities and are the main beneficiaries, receiving profits from carbon credit sales.
Local Wards
(Primarily: Baga, Mavului, Mgwashi) 		Identified through RRG’s on-the-ground partners (FOU). They own local ward land for project activities and are the main beneficiaries of carbon credit sales.
Local Community Members Identified through RRG’s on-the-ground partners (FOU). They will serve as field employees in project activities and are primary beneficiaries of profits from carbon credit sales.
